35%

Why this works: Partner commission grows each year a client renews — partners are economically motivated to stay close to clients and protect renewal. Pondera's net per assessment declines slightly Y1→Y3 ($63 less per assessment) but a retained Y3 client has delivered 3× the cumulative revenue of a churned Y1 client. The stickiness escalator costs Pondera margin at the unit level and generates it at the network level.
